About GradePath

GradePath is a simple, fast, and accurate SGPA/CGPA calculator designed for college students. It supports multiple university grading scales out of the box — including VTU (CBCS), Anna University, and a generic 10-point scale — and lets you build a custom scale if your university isn't listed.

No login required. No data leaves your browser. Everything stays on your device. Just pick your scale, enter your subjects, and see your results instantly.

Built with plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript — because a CGPA calculator doesn't need a framework. Free, open, and always will be.
